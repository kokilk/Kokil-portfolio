I am a consultant at Illinois Business Consulting, where I work on AI innovation, strategy, and workflow design to help organizations integrate emerging technologies into real business processes.

I am a Business Administration graduate from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign with experience translating complex data into actionable insights and building clear, compelling strategy narratives for decision-makers.

My work sits at the intersection of business strategy and AI systems. I design AI logic and intelligent workflows using frontier models such as GPT, Claude, and Gemini, along with workflow automation platforms like n8n to support research, strategy development, and operational efficiency.

Across my experience — from conducting market research at Curefit and managing community experiences at WeWork India, to consulting within one of the largest student-run consulting organizations in the nation — I bring a global perspective to every engagement.

I am particularly interested in strategy consulting, global markets, and the design of AI-enabled business systems, using data, automation, and intelligent workflows to solve complex business problems with clarity and impact.
